Lets get started - connect to Trello

Trello is a productivity tool that helps you to organize projects by creating individual lists, boards and cards. Manage your projects in teams, get more flexible and improve your workflow.

The Trello Calendar extends Trello and allows you to organize cards across multiple boards within one calendar view.

This calendar is open source and available at GitHub.
